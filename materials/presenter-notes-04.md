# Presenter notes - Session 4: Creative studio

Success metric: Brand HQ exists with an extracted voice guide, and the room has SEEN a landing page iterate live.

## Before the session
- Ask attendees (2 days ahead) to gather their 5 best content pieces + one screenshot of a shipped asset.
- Pre-select the campaign for Demo 2 - a real upcoming one the room cares about. Have the brief in a note ready to paste.
- Have your own Brand HQ built; if the room's Demo 1 stalls, they follow along on yours.

## Timing (45)
- 0-3 hook: paste a generic sentence, then the same sentence rewritten by your voice-guide project. "That gap is today's session."
- 3-18 concepts: voice extractor (5) · honest visual limits table (2, this buys trust) · atomization diagram + prompt (4) · critique prompt (3) · prototypes for/not-for (3).
- 18-26 Demo 1 (Brand HQ + extraction) · 26-35 Demo 2 (landing page - the crowd-pleaser; take edits by shout-out) · 35-40 Demo 3 (critique round-robin).
- 40-45 Q&A.

## Watch for
- Demo 2 is the wow moment - resist explaining; let iteration speed speak. Three room edits minimum.
- Someone will ask "can it make the images?" - the honest-limits card is your answer; pivot to "it writes the brief and critiques the results".
- C-level in the room: frame Brand HQ as brand governance (one voice, enforced by default), not as a writing tool.

## Cuts if long
- Demo 3 becomes homework. Never cut the three live iterations in Demo 2.
